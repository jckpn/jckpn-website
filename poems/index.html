10th August, 2023

He wanders aimlessly through meadows of identical flowers
Hoping for one to bring home.
Although numbingly similar, one does stand out –
A particular sunflower, its petals too vibrant to ignore.

A shade of yellow no paint could capture,
But its roots were too deep, and he means no harm.
He picks a single petal, and takes it home
And leaves it to grow for another day.
