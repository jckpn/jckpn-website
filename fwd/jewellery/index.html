About

Jewellery Making Parties Oxfordshire offers the chance to celebrate and have fun whilst being creative!

Over one and a half hours, the children will create beautiful pieces to take home using quality glass beads. We will make a bracelet and a key chain and with a wide range of beads to choose from, each child will take home unique creations to their taste.

As the sister company of Pamper Parties Oxfordshire, we have had experience of entertaining children for over 12 years. With over 1,300 successful parties to date, you can be sure that this will be a party to remember!

Stress-Free

Invitations, party bags and all equipment is provided so you can sit back and relax! The only thing you will need to prepare is food.

Memory Book Included

As well as jewellery making, we will make a memory book for the Birthday child so she/he can remember their special day!
